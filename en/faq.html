What does MG Consulting do?

MG Consulting is a training and consulting/coaching company based in Faro. You can click here to check our courses or here to learn more about us.

MG Consulting was founded in the beginning of 2015, but the research and preparation process needed to answer to company's current needs was comprehensive and took several years. If you wish to learn more about our history, click here.

What are the fundamental principles of the company?

MG Consulting stands out for its innovation, credibility, pela inovação, credibilidade, requirement and rigor; these are the values shared by all our team members. If you want to learn more about our principles, click here.

What is your target audience?

The target audience for the training courses are mostly the human resources of the companies we work with. The training sessions are mostly done in person and action-training.

What can MG Consulting do for my company?

We aim at giving our clients the tools and expertise needed for them to successfully guide and lead their companies. Furthermore, we want to increase the quality of the services given by our clients, thereby increasing their volume of business. By contributing to an increase in their professional performance by bolstering technical and social skills, we increase our client's productivity, competitiveness and their levels of efficiency and efficacy.
